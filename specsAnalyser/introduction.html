IntroductionÔÉÅ

This is an areaDetector driver for the SPECS Phobios series of electron analysers which use a Delay Line Detector (DLD)

This driver inherits from ADDriver. It implements nearly all of the parameters in asynNDArrayDriver.h and in ADArrayDriver.h. It also implements a number of parameters that are specific to the SPECS Phobios
